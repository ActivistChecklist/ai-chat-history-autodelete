Unofficial · works with claude.ai

Delete Claude chats older than X days.

You choose X (30 is the default). The extension removes every conversation past that many days—on a timer or when you hit Run. It only talks to claude.ai using your open tab. No account with us, no analytics, no servers.

Add to Chrome View code on GitHub

What we never see

  • Your settings and “how many deleted last time” sit in chrome.storage.local on this machine only.
  • We don’t get your messages, chat names, or links. At most you keep a number per run.
  • Traffic goes to claude.ai only—the same site you already use.

What you get

Run daily, weekly, monthly—or never on a timer

Until you change it, it only runs when you say so (manual). Add a schedule when you’re ready.

“Run now” anytime

Open Settings and start a pass, or use the controls on claude.ai when deletes are pending.

It doesn’t remember what you deleted

You might see something like “12 chats removed”—that’s it. No titles, no list of conversations.

How it works

  1. 1

    Install, then open claude.ai

    You must be signed in in a normal tab. The extension reuses that session—it does not log in for you.

  2. 2

    Set “older than X days” and how often

    Put in your day count. Choose a repeat schedule or leave it on manual. Turn on browser alerts if you want a nudge when deletes are waiting.

  3. 3

    It deletes, or you click Run

    On the next timed run—or when you hit Run—it wipes every chat past your day limit.
